Ratebypic is an attempt to provide a more reliable alternative to traditional review sites by letting pictures speak for themselves.

1. Reviewers frequent establishments (eg. a bar, cafe, hotel)
2. They upload a picture which accurately reflects their opinion of that establishment (eg. a cup with lipstick on it, moldy bread) combating bot influence
3. Would be customers can then view those establishments before deciding to frequent them

RateByPic is currently under development so we ask that you be patient as we work to resolve some issues.
During this period, please expect the website to experience frequent downtime.
We will inform you our pricing strategy, if and when we introduce it.
